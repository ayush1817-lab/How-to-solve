The problem

Your patient database is leaking revenue

Most clinics don’t have a patient problem—they have a follow-up problem. When communication is inconsistent, patients drift… and your diary suffers.

Leak #1

New patients vanish after the first visit

Without a structured welcome + follow-up sequence, patients don’t build momentum. They forget the plan, postpone treatment, and quietly drop off.

Leak #2

Dormant patients sit forgotten in the database

Patients who haven’t visited in 6+ months often intend to return—but nobody prompts them at the right time with the right message.

Leak #3

No time for manual follow-ups

Phone calls and ad-hoc texts don’t scale. Your team spends hours chasing, while no-shows and gaps continue.
